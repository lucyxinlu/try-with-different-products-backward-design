Try with different products

  1. Use mineral foundation. If you are worried that regular foundation will worsen dry skin or acne problems, mineral foundation may be a good alternative.
  2. Apply BB cream. BB Cream is an all-in-one cosmetic that acts as a moisturizer, primer, SPF and foundation, reducing the amount of time it takes to put on makeup.
  3. Experiment with contouring. Contouring is a makeup technique that allows you to subtly alter your face shape.
